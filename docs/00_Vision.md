# HusWood Platform Vision

## Mission

Build a modern digital platform that helps HusWood manage every stage of its business — from the first customer inquiry to completed projects and long-term client relationships.

---

## Vision

HusWood Platform is not just a CRM.

It is the digital operating system of the company.

The platform combines:

- Website
- CRM
- Project Management
- Finance
- HR
- Mobile Applications
- Client Portal
- AI Assistant

into one ecosystem.

---

## Core Principles

1. One source of truth.
2. Data is entered only once.
3. Modular architecture.
4. API First.
5. Business before code.
6. Documentation is part of the product.

---

## Long-term Goal

Create a platform that allows HusWood to grow without changing its core architecture.

Every new feature should extend the platform instead of rebuilding it.

---

## Success Criteria

The platform should allow:

- managing customers
- managing projects
- managing employees
- managing finances
- publishing website content
- communicating with clients
- scaling the business

from one integrated system.
